About me

just a little bit

I'm Julian. Im a swiss and argentinean citizen. I'm in love with the world of ideas and creativity since I can remember. My career was about being a content creator, a customer service agent, a salesman, and many more useful experiences. Furthermore, I was always combining social skills and delivering value. I've managed to work in big companies such as Citibank, GE, and Leo Messi foundation, and also in local stores and freelance projects. I started this path as a video producer while working in sales. This helped me not only to be the one who produces but also the one who sells the idea or project. Throughout this time I've designed logos, created content, and organized teams depending on the needs of the client and the project. While this happened I was always learning new tools to be able to develop all of this. I used Corel Draw in the early beginnings, Adobe Photoshop, and Sony Vegas pro for video editing then went to Premiere Pro and Fl Studio 12 for audio arrangements. But I felt something was missing. One day I entered the developer field and fell in love right away. A limitless opportunity to create and learn while staying connected to the world we live in. Nowadays I nourish by learning both front and back-end tools such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, Sass, Laravel, and so on. I've just finished my developer studies in Zurich Opportunity (a Simplon program) and looking for a fresh start where I can deliver all my best.
